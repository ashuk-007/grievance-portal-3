About Us

One-stop platform for citizens of India to seek redressal of their grievances.
The grievances can be filed under appropriate category (District Administration/Tehsil Level).
Once grievance has been submitted, a tracking number will be generated.
Citizens can track the status of the grievance with the help of the tracking number.
The grievance will be addressed by the competent authority within a period of 21 working days.

We can help you to solve your Grievances

Samadhan is an online platform available to the citizens 24x7 to lodge their grievances to the public authorities on any subject related to service delivery. It is a single portal connected to all the Ministries/Departments of Government of India and States. Every Ministry and States have role-based access to this system.
